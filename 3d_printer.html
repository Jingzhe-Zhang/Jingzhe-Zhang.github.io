Project Overview

This custom 3D printer was designed and built from scratch to address the limitations of off-the-shelf printers while incorporating advanced features. The project focused on combining cutting-edge firmware, optimized mechanics, and enhanced usability for a versatile and efficient printing solution.

Key Features

  • Klipper Firmware Integration: Wireless control and real-time adjustment capabilities enabled a 2x increase in print speed compared to similarly priced printers.
  • Chamber Heating: A custom-designed chamber heater achieves 70°C in under 10 minutes, reducing material warping by 30%, especially for high-temperature filaments like ABS and nylon.
  • Triple Z-Belt Automatic Leveling: Eliminated the need for manual bed leveling, significantly enhancing first-layer consistency and reducing setup time.
  • Compact but Efficient Design: Optimized the frame to house a 165mm x 165mm print bed within a 280mm footprint, achieving a 60% bed-to-frame ratio while maintaining rigidity.
  • Custom Motion and Thermal Management: Redesigned frame and motion system minimized print artifacts, and advanced thermal management ensured consistent print quality.

Project Overview

This project involved designing and testing a 3D-printed gearbox for mechanical performance evaluation. The process included iterative prototyping, material testing, and final optimization to ensure durability and functionality. The gearbox design focused on efficiency and reliability, using advanced CAD modeling and 3D printing technologies to create a lightweight, cost-effective solution suitable for testing and small-scale applications.

Key Features

  • Iterative Design Process: Progressed from initial CAD modeling to prototype testing and final design optimization.
  • 3D Printing for Efficiency: Utilized lightweight, cost-effective 3D-printed materials for gears and housing.
  • Functional Testing: Evaluated the gearbox for durability and performance under varying load conditions.
  • Material Selection: Focused on high-strength PLA and PETG for improved gear durability and wear resistance.

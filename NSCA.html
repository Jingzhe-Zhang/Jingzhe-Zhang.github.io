Project Overview

This project involved the design and manufacture of a composite airframe for a liquid rocket:

  • Developed modular panels for quick access to internal components, enhancing maintainability.
  • Utilized negative mold manufacturing, reducing production time by 50% and improving dimensional precision.
  • Performed structural optimization using Ansys, reducing airframe weight by 15% and improving rocket efficiency.
